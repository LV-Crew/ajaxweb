How to move components

Follow these steps below.

  • Click the component to select it. If you want to copy more than one component, press shift and then click component(s) one by one.
  • Drop your mouse to anywhere you wish and then release your mouse.
  • You can also move selected component(s) by [Up key], [Down key], [Left key], [Right key]. You can press [Ctrl] key at the same time to move component(s) slightly.
